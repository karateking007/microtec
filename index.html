What We Do

Microtec Services, Inc. offers accurate radiation contamination leak testing services, instrument calibrations, instrument repairs, and sales of new & used radiation detection equipment.

Leak Testing
Radiation Safety Officers can be confident about the integrity of sealed sources and the accuracy and completeness of their documentation.
Calibration
Experienced, fast, and competitively priced calibration service minimizes downtime and keeps survey meters accurate.
Repair
Microtec Services, Inc. calibrates and repairs most major brands of survey instruments including Victoreen, Ludlum, NDS Products, and more.
Sales
We offer a variety of new and used survey meters, rate alarms and dosimeters. We are distributors for Ludlum & NDS Products.
