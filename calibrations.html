Other Analysis Services

Emergency Analysis

For emergency situations (e.g., damaged gauges or sources, sources fished from a well, etc.) Leak tests can be sent by overnight express, analyzed immediately and results emailed to your office.

Environmental Samples

NORM samples, soil and water samples, etc. can be analyzed via high resolution gamma spectroscopy analysis or liquid scintillation (LSC) analysis. Prices quoted on request.
