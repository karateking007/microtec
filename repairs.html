Survey Meter Repair

Microtec Services, Inc. calibrates and repairs most major brands of survey instruments including Victoreen, Ludlum, Eberline, G.E. Smith, NDS Products, Eon, Technical Associates Gamma Industries, Wm. B. Johnson, Bicron, Industrial Nuclear, Dosimeter Corporation. If your brand of instrument is not listed, please call us for further information.

If repair work is required, an estimate can be provided before any work is performed. If necessary, batteries are automatically replaced. New and used survey instruments available to meet all of your survey and monitoring needs. See 'New Instruments' section for available brands and models.

Due to the complexity of today's survey instruments (multiple detectors, microprocessor controlled instruments, instruments requiring infra-red communication devices, etc.), please call for quotations on your particular instrument.
